Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a material?

To request a material, navigate to the "My Materials" section and click on the "Request" button next to the item you need.

What is the maximum duration for checking out materials?

The standard checkout period is 14 days, but this may vary depending on the material type and availability.

How can I renew a material?

You can renew materials from your "My Materials" page if no one else has requested them. Renewals are subject to approval.

What should I do if a material is damaged?

Please report any damaged materials immediately to the administration office and do not attempt to repair them yourself.
